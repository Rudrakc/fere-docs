## Fere Pro

The Pro Agent is a state-of-the-art AI agent that can perform a variety of tasks, including:

1. Searching for coins based on themes, chains, investors, market cap, and trading volume.
2. Providing historical and current market data, including price, market cap, volume change, price change, sentiment change, etc.
3. Analyzing a coin's social media activity on Twitter and Farcaster.
4. Finding the latest news about a specific coin.
5. Performing searches for casts by a Farcaster user.
6. Searching for tweets by a Twitter user.
7. Searching and analyzing casts within a Farcaster channel.
8. Identifying key opinion leaders (KOLs) for a coin.
9. Generating code for NFT projects or meme coins.
10. Answering general questions about real-world, real-time crypto events or news.

:::tip[Search Syntax for specific usage]

**Username**

Use `@` syntax to search for a username. Example: `@dwr.eth` or `@elonmusk`.

The agent will automatically search for the user on Twitter or Farcaster. To specify a particular social network, include it in your query.

**Farcaster Channel**

Use `/` syntax to search for casts in a Farcaster channel.

Example: "What's the latest in /farcaster?"

**Coins**

Prepend `$` to the token name for better results.

Example: Use `$DEGEN` instead of `DEGEN`.

:::

## Investment Agent

:::info

The investment agent is in beta and not rolled out to everyone.
We are making it better and will be available for everyone soon
:::

The Investment Agent is an advanced AI agent designed to invest your crypto in liquidity pools. It operates 24/7, automatically collecting fees, withdrawing, and balancing your portfolio.

### Start Investing

To start investing:

1. Connect your wallet to the Investment Agent.
2. Enter the amount you wish to invest. The minimum investment requirement is 20 USD.
3. Click on "Invest," which will prompt your wallet for confirmation.
4. Once the transaction is confirmed, your balance will be displayed on the screen.
5. Click on "Start." The agent will take a few minutes to find the best pool and invest in it.

You can revisit this screen at any time to see your current portfolio value.

### Taking Returns

1. Connect your wallet to the Investment Agent. Funds will be transferred to the connected wallet.
2. Click on "Stop." The agent will take a few minutes to halt operations.
3. Once stopped, click on the "Withdraw" button.
4. The portfolio amount will be transferred to your connected wallet.

### How does it work?

1. The agent identifies the best liquidity pools on Uniswap V3.
2. It invests your funds in the selected pool.
3. If the pool's APY decreases or the token's price declines, the agent moves your funds to the next best liquidity pool.
4. Throughout this process, the agent continuously collects fees and rewards.

### What is the fee?

The investment fee is 1% or USD 1.00, whichever is higher. Additionally, 5% of the profits earned is charged.
