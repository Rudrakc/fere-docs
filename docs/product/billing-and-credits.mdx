## Credits

Every interaction with Pro Agent or Market Pulse Agent requires credits.

:::info

You get a sign-up bonus of 200 credits. This helps you get started with FereAI.

:::

| Agent Name | Credits |
|------------|---------|
| Fere Pro  | 15      |
| Market Pulse Agent | 70 |
| Investment Agent | 0 |

The credits used by an Agent are also displayed in the top right corner of the chat window.

## How do I earn credits for free?

You can earn credits by inviting your friends to FereAI. You can earn 200 credits for every friend who signs up using your
referral code. Your friend also gets 200 credits as a sign-up bonus.

## How to check credits?

Your credits are displayed in the bottom right corner of the chat window.

## How to check billing history?

You can check your billing history by clicking on the "Billing" section and then going to "Billing History".
